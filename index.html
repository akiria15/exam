Some Things About My Favorite Sport

Why soccer?

Here are some thing that makes soccer a great sport and reasons why it is my favorite.

  • Great form of exercise
  • Increases eye leg/foot coordination
  • Has low risks of being injured
  • Can be a lifelong skill

Different Positions

The different positions are defense, midfield, goalkeeper, and fowards. Only the goal keeper is allowed to touch the ball with their hands, any other player can not.

How To Play

The goalkeeper or defense player is expected to be between the ball and the goal at all times ready to stop the ball from going in.

How To Play

Midfield players cover the most ground during a game and should have a high level of fitness. A foward takes shots at the goal.

Soccer Teams

a game played by two teams of eleven players with a round ball that may not be touched with the hands or arms during play except by the goalkeepers. The object of the game is to score goals by kicking or heading the ball into the goal of the opponents.
